Join or Renew Today!

Joining as an AHA/ASA Professional Member puts you in touch with more than 32,000 like-minded individuals and brings you closer to the heart and soul of the AHA and its mission of building healthier lives, free of cardiovascular diseases and stroke.

Become A Member Renew Your Membership

Access to our career enhancing benefits, including:

  • Up to $400 in savings on Scientific Sessions registration and International Stroke Conference registration plus savings at AHA’s other scientific conferences
  • Customized access to research and clinical information across multiple specialties
  • Leadership Opportunities
  • Be a part of our AHA Professional Online Network — connect with peers and leaders in your specialty
  • Research grant application fee waived for members. You can apply at my.americanheart.org/research
  • Online access to AHA scientific journals
  • Affiliate with our Scientific Councils
